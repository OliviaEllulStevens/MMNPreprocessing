## MMN Preprocessing
Adapting the [LuckLab ERPCORE EEG Preprocessing Pipeline](https://github.com/lucklab/ERP_CORE) to find MMN and practice with pipeline execution. 

### What Have I Learnt From This Experience?
- Practice with EEG Preprocessing and experimental design.
- Deeper understanding of Mismatch Negativity and Event-Related Potentials.
- Greater knowledge of MATLAB.

### What Scripts Did I Adapt?
- MMN_RecChannels-OliviaScripts - includes all preprocessing stages and reccomended channels in the original LuckLab Pipeline, adapted to fit our Passive Auditory Oddball study.
- MMN_Experimental-OliviaScripts - includes all preprocessing stages outlined in the original LuckLab Pipeline, with altered channels and methods to experiment with preprocessing!

All Scripts contain notations of useful syntax and methodology, for my own learning. I have found this experience very useful for future work with preprocessing pipelines!
